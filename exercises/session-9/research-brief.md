# Research Brief: Patient Readmission Prediction

## The Vague Request

> "We've been collecting patient data for years but never really looked at what predicts readmission. Can you figure out which patients are most likely to come back within 30 days? Leadership wants to reduce readmission rates."

## Questions to Research

1. What is the standard definition of "readmission" in healthcare analytics?
2. What ML approaches are commonly used (LACE index, HOSPITAL score)?
3. What are the most predictive features (diagnosis, length of stay, demographics)?
4. What is an appropriate performance threshold for clinical decision support?
5. Are there fairness considerations (disparate impact across demographics)?
6. What regulatory context exists (CMS Hospital Readmissions Reduction Program)?
7. How should the model be validated for clinical deployment?

## Expected Analysis Plan Sections

Your completed plan should address:
- Problem definition and business impact
- Data requirements and availability
- Feature engineering strategy
- Model selection rationale
- Validation approach (temporal validation for clinical models)
- Fairness and bias assessment
- Deployment considerations
- Success metrics (both statistical and business)
