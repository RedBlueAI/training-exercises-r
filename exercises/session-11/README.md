# Session 11: Measuring Impact & ROI

**Duration:** ~25 minutes
**Focus:** Quantifying the impact of AI-assisted data science

## Learning Objectives

- Measure before/after performance with AI assistance
- Calculate ROI for AI tools in data science workflows
- Build a business case for AI-assisted analytics

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Before/After Time Trial (15 min)

**Task:** Create a summary statistics table grouped by heart disease status

**Round 1 - No AI (7 min):**
Write R code manually to:
- Group heart data by heart_disease
- Calculate mean, sd, median for age, cholesterol, max_heart_rate
- Format as a presentation-ready table
- Time yourself and note: lines written, bugs encountered, documentation lookups

**Round 2 - With AI (5 min):**
```
/ImplementFeature "Create a publication-ready summary statistics table comparing clinical measurements between heart disease positive and negative groups, with proper formatting and statistical tests"
```
- Time yourself and note: lines generated, quality of output, customization needed

**Compare:** Fill in the ROI worksheet.

### Exercise 2: Data Science ROI Projection (10 min)

Using the worksheet in `roi-worksheet.md`, calculate:

1. **EDA Time Savings**: How much faster is AI-assisted EDA?
2. **Code Quality**: Does AI produce more reproducible code?
3. **Statistical Rigor**: Does AI catch more assumption violations?
4. **Documentation**: How much time saved on reporting?
5. **Onboarding**: How much faster can a new analyst get productive?

## Key Metrics for Data Science Teams

| Activity | Without AI | With AI | Savings |
|----------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Initial EDA | 4-8 hours | 1-2 hours | 60-75% |
| Model building | 2-4 days | 0.5-1 day | 60-75% |
| Code review | 2-4 hours | 30-60 min | 70-80% |
| Report writing | 4-6 hours | 1-2 hours | 60-70% |
| Test writing | 2-3 hours | 30-60 min | 70-80% |
| Debugging | 1-3 hours | 15-45 min | 60-75% |
